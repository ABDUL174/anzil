What I Do

What I Provide For You

Dental Care

Our dental services cover everything from routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced cosmetic and restorative procedures. We focus on maintaining optimal oral health while enhancing the natural beauty of your smile. Treatments include teeth whitening, veneers, crowns, bridges, root canals, dental implants, and smile designing — all performed with precision, care, and the latest technology.

GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) Therapy

GFC therapy is a revolutionary treatment that utilizes your own blood’s growth factors, extracted and concentrated to promote natural healing and regeneration. It is highly effective in treating hair thinning and hair loss, especially in early-stage alopecia, by stimulating dormant hair follicles. GFC is also used in skin rejuvenation to reduce fine lines, improve texture, and restore a youthful glow — all without synthetic chemicals or surgery.

Cosmeceuticals

Cosmeceuticals bridge the gap between cosmetics and pharmaceuticals, offering advanced skincare products that deliver medical-grade results. These formulations are rich in active ingredients like peptides, antioxidants, retinoids, and acids that target specific skin concerns such as acne, hyperpigmentation, aging, and dullness. Customized for individual skin types and needs, our cosmeceutical treatments provide safe, science-backed solutions for long-term skin health.

MNRF (Microneedling Radiofrequency)

Microneedling Radiofrequency is a cutting-edge, non-surgical aesthetic procedure that combines the benefits of microneedling with the deep-penetrating effects of radiofrequency energy. This dual action stimulates collagen production and tightens the skin from within. It is particularly effective for acne scars, enlarged pores, stretch marks, sagging skin, and fine lines. MNRF promotes smoother, firmer, and more youthful skin with minimal downtime.

Chemical Peels

A skin resurfacing treatment that uses safe, medical-grade chemical solutions to exfoliate the top layers of skin. Chemical peels help reduce pigmentation, acne, fine lines, and uneven skin tone. By stimulating cell turnover, they reveal smoother, brighter, and clearer skin, and can be customized for different skin types and concerns.

Mesotherapy

A minimally invasive technique that delivers a customized blend of vitamins, enzymes, hyaluronic acid, and other active ingredients directly into the middle layer of the skin. Mesotherapy is used for skin hydration, brightening, pigmentation reduction, and hair restoration. It improves skin texture, elasticity, and overall radiance with minimal downtime.
